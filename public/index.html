Important issue

  1. Lighting conditions
    • Avoid strong backlighting or light spots
    • Keep the light even; soft light or natural diffused light is recommended
  2. Background selection
    • Use a solid, non-textured background (e.g., white or grey)
    • Prevent the background from being too close to the color of the LEGOs
  3. Camera angle
    • It is recommended to shoot vertically and keep the plane of the LEGO model parallel to the lens
    • Avoid too much tilting or yawing
  4. Resolution and focus
    • Ensure the camera or phone focuses accurately on the LEGO studs and that their edges are clear
    • Use the highest possible resolution to capture fine details
